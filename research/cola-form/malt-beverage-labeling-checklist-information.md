CHECKLIST OF MANDATORY LABEL INFORMATION | MALT BEVERAGE

This guidance lists the mandatory information that TTB reviews on every malt beverage certificate of label approval (COLA) application and includes a checklist to help prevent common errors.

This document does not provide comprehensive labeling guidance.

Malt beverages for which a COLA is required must be labeled in compliance with all applicable requirements in 27 CFR part 7, 27 CFR part 16, and 27 CFR part 25.
